## 2.6 Constraints

### Technical Constraints
- What technology platforms must be used? (required technologies)
- What platforms are not allowed? (technology restrictions)
- What existing systems must be integrated with?
- What technical standards must be followed?
- What infrastructure limitations exist?

### Budget Constraints
- What is the maximum budget?
- Are there cost per user/transaction limits?
- What are the ongoing operational cost constraints?
- Are there capital vs operational expense restrictions?

### Licensing Constraints
- What licensing models are acceptable?
- Are there restrictions on open-source usage?
- What vendor relationships exist that impact licensing?
- Are there seat/user licensing constraints?

### Timeline Constraints
- What is the project deadline?
- What are the phase/milestone deadlines?
- Are there regulatory compliance deadlines?
- What seasonal/business cycle constraints exist?

### Team/Resource Constraints
- What is the team size available?
- What skill gaps exist in the team?
- What training budget/time is available?
- Are there hiring restrictions?

Format: For each constraint, specify if it is a hard constraint (must follow) or soft constraint (preferred).
