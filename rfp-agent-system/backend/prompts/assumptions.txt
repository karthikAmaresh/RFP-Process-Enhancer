## 4. Assumptions and Dependencies

### Project Assumptions
- What is assumed about the current environment?
- What capabilities are assumed to exist?
- What resources are assumed to be available?
- What organizational readiness is assumed?
- What data quality/availability is assumed?

### Technical Dependencies
- What external systems must be available?
- What APIs or services are required?
- What data feeds are needed?
- What infrastructure is required?
- What third-party tools are needed?

### Vendor Dependencies
- What third-party vendors are involved?
- What SaaS services are required?
- What licensing agreements are needed?
- What support contracts are required?

### Organizational Dependencies
- What approvals are needed?
- What organizational changes must happen?
- What training must be completed?
- What process changes are required?

### Timeline Dependencies
- What must be completed before the project starts?
- What parallel projects impact this work?
- What milestones depend on external factors?

### Data Dependencies
- What data must be available?
- What data migrations are required?
- What data quality improvements are needed?

Format: For each assumption, note if it has been validated or is unvalidated. For each dependency, note the risk if it's not available.
