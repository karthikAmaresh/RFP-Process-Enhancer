## 2.8 Non-Functional Requirements

### Performance Requirements
- **Response Time:** What are the acceptable response times? (e.g., < 2 seconds)
- **Throughput:** What transaction volumes must be supported? (e.g., 10,000 TPS)
- **Latency:** What are the latency requirements for API calls?
- **Page Load Time:** What are the page load time expectations?
- **Batch Processing:** What are the batch job completion time requirements?

### Scalability Requirements
- **User Scalability:** How many concurrent users?
- **Data Scalability:** What is the expected data growth? (volume over time)
- **Transaction Scalability:** How do transaction volumes grow?
- **Horizontal Scaling:** Can the system scale out?
- **Vertical Scaling:** What are the resource scaling requirements?

### Security Requirements
- **Authentication:** What authentication methods are required? (SSO, MFA, LDAP)
- **Authorization:** What RBAC/ABAC model is needed?
- **Encryption:** What encryption is required? (at rest, in transit, key management)
- **Audit Logging:** What security audit requirements exist?
- **Vulnerability Management:** What security scanning/testing is required?
- **Data Privacy:** What PII/sensitive data protection is needed?

### Usability Requirements
- **User Interface:** What UI/UX standards must be met?
- **Accessibility:** What accessibility standards? (WCAG 2.1 AA, Section 508)
- **Browser Support:** What browsers must be supported?
- **Mobile Support:** Is mobile/responsive design required?
- **Internationalization:** What languages/locales are needed?

### Reliability Requirements
- **Failure Recovery:** What is the MTTR (Mean Time To Recovery)?
- **Data Integrity:** What data consistency guarantees are needed?
- **Error Handling:** What error recovery mechanisms are required?
- **Redundancy:** What redundancy is needed?

### Availability Requirements
- **Uptime SLA:** What availability percentage? (99.9%, 99.99%)
- **Planned Downtime:** What maintenance windows are acceptable?
- **Business Hours:** Is 24/7 availability needed?
- **Regional Availability:** What geographic availability is required?

### Disaster Recovery Requirements
- **RPO (Recovery Point Objective):** How much data loss is acceptable?
- **RTO (Recovery Time Objective):** How quickly must systems recover?
- **Backup Frequency:** How often must backups occur?
- **DR Site:** Is a disaster recovery site required?
- **Failover:** What failover capabilities are needed?

### Compliance and Regulatory Requirements
- **Standards:** What standards must be met? (ISO 27001, SOC 2, PCI-DSS)
- **Regulations:** What regulations apply? (GDPR, HIPAA, SOX, CCPA)
- **Certifications:** What certifications are required?
- **Audit Requirements:** What audit trails and reports are needed?
- **Data Residency:** Where must data be stored? (geographic restrictions)

Format: Always include specific metrics, percentages, and thresholds when mentioned. If not specified, note "To be determined" for critical NFRs.
